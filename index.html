HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

WASD / Arrow Keys - Move your character

SPACE - Basic attack (FREE - no MP cost!)

R - Special AOE attack (costs 10 MP, hits 3x3 area)

F - Wait/Defend (+50% DEF this turn, advances enemies, stacks Bulwark/Iron Roots)

E - Use Health Potion (restores 50 HP & 25 MP)

Click Items - Equip/unequip gear from inventory

OBJECTIVE

• Explore the dungeon and defeat enemies to gain XP and loot

• Reach the golden stairs to descend to the next floor

• Each floor gets harder but drops better loot

• Survive as long as possible!

EQUIPMENT SYSTEM

8 Equipment Slots: Weapon, Helmet, Chest, Legs, Gloves, Boots, Cape, Relic

• Each piece provides ATK and/or DEF bonuses

• Higher rarity = better stats and more passive effects

• Right-click items to discard them

• Discard 10 of same rarity to craft next tier up!

PASSIVE EFFECTS & AFFINITIES

Rare+ items have passive buffs organized into 5 Affinities. Every other level, you choose an Affinity to invest in — higher Affinity = more likely gear drops with those buffs!

Affinities also grant milestone bonuses at levels 5 and 10:

ATK: Lv 5: +5% base damage | Lv 10: +10% base damage

DEF: Lv 5: +5% base DEF | Lv 10: +10% base DEF

SPD: Lv 5: +1 move speed | Lv 10: +1 move speed

CRIT: Lv 5: +10% crit damage | Lv 10: +20% crit damage

LUCK: Lv 5: +5% rarity bonus | Lv 10: +10% rarity bonus

Plus Vampiric and Arcane (utility, no affinity)

LEGENDARY+ EXCLUSIVE PASSIVES

These powerful effects only appear on Legendary, Mythic, and Ascended gear:

• Soulrend - Attacks ignore % of enemy DEF

• Undying - Survive a lethal hit with 1 HP (once per floor)

• Speedster - 20% base speed + 50% bonus damage from speed

• Kinetic Reserve - Moving without attacking: +60% damage per stack on next hit (max 4 stacks → up to 3.4x)

• Goldblood - Luck also boosts your ATK and DEF

• Siphon - Kills restore MP

• Psychic Flare - AOE range +1 tile, costs 5 more MP

• Doom Aura - Nearby enemies take scaling damage each turn

ASCENDED MEDALLIONS

Discard 10 Ascended items to forge a permanent Medallion with one of these powers:

• Double Up - Equip additional Weapon, Cape, and Relic

• Lacerating Blows - All hits crit + bleed (1/5 maxHP for 3 turns)

• Time Dilation - Speed always 1.5x faster than enemies

• 8-Leaf Clover - Items always spawn with max values

• Soul Forge - Discarding items grants +1 to a random base stat

• Chrono Strike - Every 4th attack hits twice (2nd hit at 50% damage)

MASTERY PASSIVES

When you reach Affinity level 8 in two or three stats, powerful Mastery passives unlock in the loot pool. These only appear on Legendary, Mythic, or Ascended gear.

Dual Mastery — requires level 8 in two Affinities:

• War Machine (ATK+DEF) — Damage adds 25% of total DEF. Attacks bypass Phase Shift

• Berserker's Fury (ATK+SPD) — Kills grant +3% ATK (max +30%). Kills grant 1 free move

• Lethal Precision (ATK+CRIT) — Crits deal 3x base. Non-crits deal +15% bonus

• Blood Tithe (ATK+LUCK) — 25% potion on kill. Overkill heals 10% of overkill

• Living Fortress (DEF+SPD) — Hits dealing <10% max HP deal 0. Taking 0 grants +2 SPD

• Iron Retaliation (DEF+CRIT) — Counterattack for 100% DEF when hit. Can crit

• Providence (DEF+LUCK) — 20% chance to halve incoming damage. Gear gets +1 passive slot

• Flash Point (SPD+CRIT) — 1st attack each floor is guaranteed crit. Every 3rd attack crits

• Treasure Sense (SPD+LUCK) — Hidden treasure tiles per floor. SPD increases detection range. Drop Rare+ gear

• Fortune's Edge (CRIT+LUCK) — 30% chance crit kills upgrade dropped gear's rarity

Tri Mastery — requires level 8 in three Affinities:

• Warlord (ATK+DEF+SPD) — ATK/DEF gain +15% of each other's average. +1 SPD per 50 ATK+DEF

• Deathbringer (ATK+DEF+CRIT) — 2x damage to enemies below 50% HP. Killing tough enemies heals 25%

• Pirate King (ATK+DEF+LUCK) — All drops guaranteed Rare+. Bosses always drop 2 items

• Storm Dancer (ATK+SPD+CRIT) — Move 3+ tiles, next attack is AOE crit in 2-tile radius. 5-turn CD

• Gambler's Ruin (ATK+SPD+LUCK) — 10% for 3x damage, 5% for 0. Free moves spawn gold tiles (+2 ATK)

• Bastion (DEF+SPD+CRIT) — Moving through enemies deals 50% DEF. Can crit + stun 1 turn

• Lucky Star (DEF+SPD+LUCK) — 30% potion on dodge. Survive lethal via teleport (1/floor)

• Assassin's Creed (ATK+CRIT+LUCK) — Crits ignore 100% DEF. Doubled crit chance vs full HP enemies

• Mirage (SPD+CRIT+LUCK) — 15% attacks phase through. After phasing, next crit deals +50%

• Chaos Engine (DEF+CRIT+LUCK) — Random stat +10% each turn. Blocking counterattacks + stuns

ENDGAME SCALING (Floor 35+)

As you descend deeper, the dungeon fights back:

• Floor Modifiers — Every 5 floors past 35, enemies gain random modifiers (Ironclad, Frenzied, Vampiric Horde, Phase Shift, Convergence)

• Player Debuffs — Permanent debuffs stack at thresholds: Corrosion (F35), Fatigue (F40), Entropy (F45), Cursed Blood (F50), Withering (F55), Void Siphon (F60), Death's Countdown (F65)

• ELITE enemies appear past floor 15 with personal modifiers and better loot

• ARCANE WARD enemies appear past floor 25 — resist 75% of AOE damage, must be killed with melee!

• Mana Burn — Casting AOE without moving costs +3 MP each consecutive cast. Move to reset!

• SACRIFICE ALTARS appear past floor 60 — sacrifice equipped gear for permanent stat gains

TIPS

• Pink enemies are BOSSES - they drop better loot!

• GOLD enemies are RARE BOSSES - appear past floor 5, extremely dangerous!

• MAGENTA enemies are ELITES - appear past floor 15, have personal modifiers!

• PURPLE enemies are ARCANE WARDS - appear past floor 25, resist AOE, melee only!

• Floors beyond 8 spawn additional bosses - be prepared!

• Enemies drop Health Potions - stock up before going deeper

• Equip gear for all 8 slots to maximize your power

• Farm bosses for legendary items with multiple passives

• Use special attacks wisely - MP is valuable!

• Hover over items to compare with equipped gear

• When you level up, choose stat points. Every other level, also choose an Affinity!

• Luck improves loot drop quality - invest wisely!

• RED PORTALS warp 10 floors ahead: +2 ATK, DEF, SPD, CRIT, HP, MP, Luck + 1 Affinity point

• BLUE PORTALS warp 30 floors ahead: +3 ATK, DEF, SPD, CRIT, HP, MP, +7 Luck + 2 Affinity points

• Portals are the only way to increase your Luck stat during a run — plan accordingly!

• Legendary+ gear can roll exclusive powerful passives!

• Reach 80+ Luck to unlock Ascended item drops!

• Discard 10 Ascended items to forge an Ascended Medallion!

• Past floor 65, Death's Countdown gives you 40 turns per floor!
